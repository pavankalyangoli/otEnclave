Below is a list of some of the dedicated services offered by OT Enclave aimed at protecting and securing OT/ICS environments from cyber threats

Risk Assessment and Analysis

assess the cybersecurity risks, identify vulnerabilities, potential attack vectors, and weak points in the industrial control systems.

Security Architecture Design and Implementation

Design and deploy robust security architectures tailored to OT environment, including, implementing firewalls, intrusion detection/prevention systems, network segmentation, and access controls.

Network Monitoring and Incident Response

Set up continuous monitoring of industrial networks to detect and respond to any unusual or malicious activities. Develop incident response plans to minimize the impact of cyber incidents and facilitate rapid recovery.

Vulnerability Management

Identify, prioritize, and manage vulnerabilities in the industrial environment's hardware, software, and configurations, involving regular patch management and updates.

Endpoint Security

Deploy security solutions to protect individual endpoints within the OT network, such as industrial devices and controllers, against malware and unauthorized access.

Security Awareness Training

Provide training, raising awareness about cybersecurity best practices and help in recognizing and responding to potential threats.

Compliance and Regulatory Support

Assist in complying with industry-specific regulations and standards related to cybersecurity, such as NIST, IEC 62443, and ISO 27001.

Secure Remote Access Solutions

With the rise of remote work and maintenance, we develop secure remote access solutions that allow authorized personnel to access and manage industrial systems from remote locations while maintaining security.

Penetration Testing and Red Teaming

Conduct controlled ethical hacking exercises to identify vulnerabilities and weaknesses in the OT environment, help in improving defenses.

About Us

Our deep understanding of IACS (Industrial Automation and Control systems), SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) systems, and other operational technologies allows us to provide comprehensive security solutions tailored specifically to your organization's needs.
